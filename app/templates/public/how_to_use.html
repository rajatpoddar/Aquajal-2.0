How to Use Aquajal

For Managers

1. Setting Up Your Business
  • Price Settings: Go to `Manager Menu > Price Settings` to set the default prices for new jars and dispensers.
  • Manage Staff: Use the `Manager Menu > Manage Staff` option to add, edit, or remove staff members. You can set their daily wages here.
  • Manage Stock: In the `Manager Dashboard`, click on `Manage Stock` to add new inventory of jars and dispensers.
2. Daily Operations
  • Customer Management: From the `Manager Menu`, select `Customers` to add new customers or update details of existing ones.
  • Event Bookings: New event bookings from customers or staff will appear on your dashboard. You can confirm them, which will then deduct the stock.
  • Receiving Cash: At the end of the day, when a staff member hands over their collected cash, you can record it from the dashboard to reset their balance.

For Staff (Delivery Personnel)

1. Logging Deliveries

On your dashboard, use the search bar to find a customer by name or mobile number. Once you find them, enter the number of jars delivered and click `Deliver`.

2. Managing Expenses

If you incur any expenses, like for fuel, you can add them on your dashboard. This amount will be deducted from your cash balance.

For Customers

As a customer, you can log in to view your delivery history, request additional jars for daily use, or book jars in bulk for an event.
