How to Use Aquajal

For Managers

1. Setting Up Your Business
  • Initial Setup: After registering, your first step is to set your business's default prices and attendance rules. Go to `Manager Menu > Price Settings` to set prices for new jars/dispensers and define the minimum jar deliveries for a full or half day's wage.
  • Manage Staff: Use the `Manager Menu > Manage Staff` option to add your delivery personnel. You can set their daily wages and even upload ID proofs.
  • Add Customers: From the `Manager Menu`, select `Customers` to build your customer list. Here you can set custom jar prices and add special notes for each customer.
2. Daily Operations
  • Stock Management: Keep your inventory updated from the `Manager Dashboard` by clicking on `Manage Stock`. Stock is automatically deducted for sales and confirmed event bookings.
  • Confirm Bookings: New event bookings will appear on your dashboard. Confirm them to allocate stock and set the final amount.
  • Receive Cash: At the end of the day, use the `Receive Cash` button on your dashboard to record cash received from staff and reset their balance.
3. Invoicing & Reports
  • View All Invoices: Go to `Manager Menu > Invoices` to see a list of all invoices generated. You can view, download as PDF, or email them to customers directly.
  • Generate Reports: The `Reports` button on your dashboard takes you to a powerful reporting tool. View monthly summaries of your income, expenses, and staff attendance, or drill down into daily transaction logs.

For Staff (Delivery Personnel)

1. Your Dashboard is Your Hub

Your dashboard is designed for quick actions. You can see your current cash balance, log deliveries, record expenses, and see pending event deliveries for the day.

2. Logging a Delivery

Use the search bar to find a customer by name or mobile number. Once they appear, enter the number of jars delivered. If the customer isn't paying immediately, check the "Mark as Due" box before clicking `Deliver`.

3. Selling New Products

You can sell a new jar or dispenser to a walk-in customer using the `Sell New Jar / Dispenser` button. The amount will be added to your cash balance and stock will be updated automatically.

For Customers

As a customer, your personal dashboard allows you to manage your account easily. You can:

  • View your complete activity history, including past deliveries and event bookings.
  • See any outstanding due amounts.
  • View and download all your past invoices from the `Invoices` tab.
  • Quickly request extra jars for daily use or book jars in bulk for an upcoming event.
