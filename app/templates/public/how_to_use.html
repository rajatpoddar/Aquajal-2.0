How to Use Aquajal

A step-by-step guide for each role in the Aquajal ecosystem.

  1. Register Your Business: Sign up and create your business profile. You'll get a 40-day free trial to explore all features.
  2. Set Up Your Business: From the Manager Dashboard, go to "Price Settings" to set prices for new jars and dispensers, and define your low-stock alert thresholds.
  3. Add Staff: Navigate to "Manage Staff" to add your delivery personnel. Set their wages and upload ID proofs.
  4. Manage Customers: Use the "Customers" menu to add or view your customer list, track their dues, and generate monthly invoices.
  5. Oversee Operations: Your dashboard gives you a real-time overview of staff cash balances, pending event bookings, and total customer dues.
  6. Procure Supplies: Running low on stock? Click "Procure Supplies" to browse products from various suppliers and place an order directly.

  3. Record Expenses: Incurred an expense during your delivery? Log it instantly through your dashboard.
  4. Cash Handover: At the end of the day, your dashboard shows your total cash balance. Hand it over to your manager, who will mark it as received.
  5. Event Deliveries: Your manager will assign you event deliveries. Once delivered, mark the status as "Delivered" from your dashboard.
